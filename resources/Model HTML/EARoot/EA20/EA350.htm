Used to request flow transactions. Client EndDevices submit a request for charging or discharging from the server. The server creates an associated FlowReservationResponse containing the charging parameters and interval to provide a lower aggregated demand at the premises, or within a larger part of the distribution system. <br/>

Notes: Indicates the total amount of energy, in Watt-Hours, requested to be transferred between the storage device and the electric power system.
