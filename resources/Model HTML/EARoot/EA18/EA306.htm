A Billing Period relates to the period of time on which a customer is billed.  As an example the billing period interval for a particular customer might be 31 days starting on July 1, 2011. The start date and interval can change on each billing period. There may also be multiple billing periods related to a customer agreement to support different tariff structures.<br/>
